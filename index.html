WHY I'M RUNNING

“With our vast natural resources, our fertile lands, our people, our eight and four-year colleges and universities, our natural beauty and historic landmarks, Laikipia should be in the top when it comes to economic growth — not the bottom five.”

“Born and raised in the Laikipia County, I continue to make and impact within the community. Putting what’s best for our county ahead of partisanship being my priority.
After the collapse of Mountex that costed us thousands of jobs. We'll fight to bring and build industries back to Laikipia County, and make our county appealing for us and our generations.”

Why Money Market Funds Are Safe: The Role of Maturity, Credit Ratings, and Stress Tests

  • by Moses Wanjema
  • Apr 21, 2024
  • Comments

Money market funds (MMFs) are a popular investment choice due to their stability, liquidity, and modest returns. Their safety is primarily attributed to strict regulatory oversight and risk management practices. These funds invest in short-term, high-quality securities and undergo stress testing to ensure they can withstand market fluctuations, making them a secure option for investors.
